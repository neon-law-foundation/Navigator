Welcome to the Neon Law Foundation blog.

The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit working to close the justice gap
through AI, automation, and continuing legal education. This blog is where
we'll share what we're learning as we build: product updates, notes from our
CLE work, and honest write-ups of what does and doesn't work when you try to
put modern tooling into the hands of legal aid attorneys and pro bono
volunteers.

A few things to expect:

- **Short posts, often.** We'd rather publish a specific observation than
  sit on a long essay.
- **Code and citations.** When we claim something works, we'll show the
  receipts — code, studies, or both.
- **No vendor pitches.** The Foundation doesn't sell software. When we
  recommend a tool, it's because it helped a real attorney serve a real
  client.

Thanks for reading. If there's a topic you'd like us to cover, email
<support@neonlaw.org>.
