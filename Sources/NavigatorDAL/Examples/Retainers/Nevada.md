provided under it — including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination —
shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered under the rules of the American Arbitration
Association ("AAA") then in effect. The arbitration shall take place in Nevada. The arbitrator's
award shall be final and binding, and judgment may be entered upon it in any court of competent
jurisdiction. You waive any right to a jury trial for claims covered by this clause.

## Data Deletion and Release of Claims

If you request that Neon Law delete your data, we will honor that request.
However, you must understand the following consequence before making that
request:

**Once your data is deleted, it is gone permanently. We will have no records,
documents, communications, or other information relating to your matter. If you
later assert any claim, complaint, or legal action against Neon Law arising out
of or related to the services we provided to you, we will be unable to defend
ourselves because we will have none of your data.**

By initialing below, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this

have against Neon Law arising out of or related to the services covered by this
